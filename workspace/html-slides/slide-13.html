Smoothing & Daily Change Features

After interpolation we smooth each depth with a 20-day Savitzky–Golay filter so the signal reflects crop-scale trends instead of probe jitter.

ΔVWC captures how quickly the profile is drying or being refilled.

  • Positive ΔVWC flags irrigation or rain recharge events.
  • Negative ΔVWC matches evapotranspiration drawdown between irrigations.
